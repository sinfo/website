How to win prizes?

ATTEND ANY SESSION

Company Presentation | Lunch Talk | Speaker Keynote | Workshop

We'll giveaway one prize per individual session to one lucky attendant. To qualify, you'll need to be attending the session until its end — we'll reveal the lucky winner at the end of it.

WIN POINTS ON THE SCOREBOARD

Win points by attending any session and staying on the daily top 3

Win as many points as you can to stay on our top 3! You can win points by attending a session, being on speed dating calls, visiting stands, or by scanning surprise giveaway codes!

DAILY LOTTERY

Win a prize even if you're not on the top 3 on the scoreboard

If you're not on the top 3 on the scoreboard, you can still win a prize on our daily lottery. Your number of points are worth as “entries” — more points, more chances to win the prize!

SUBMIT YOUR CURRICULUM

Enter to win our biggest giveaway by submitting your curriculum

Do you want to win a brand new PlayStation 5? All you have to do is submit your curriculum vitae on our web app, and you'll be automatically entered to win!

DON'T FORGET TO ENTER THE GIVEAWAY CODES!

To get a chance to win our giveaways, you'll need to enter the unique codes that will be sent during sessions. These will show up in the 'Chat' section at random times, so be sure to be paying attention!

To enter the codes, just open our web app, hop on the "Self Check In" tab and enter them — simple as that!
